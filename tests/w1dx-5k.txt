; Initial callsign
MPW 53 40 40 40 "W1DX" 40 40 *
; Normal callsign 
MPW 52 40 40 40 "W1DX" 40 40 *
; Lock control operator password from control operator use
MPW 94 92 1 *
; Lock master password from control operator use
MPW 94 93 1 *
; 600 Hz CW tone
MPW 06 16 67 *
; ID interval (10 mins)
MPW 51 99 * 
; Carrier access 
MPW 57 00 1 *
; Carrier + PL access 
MPW 57 00 3 *
